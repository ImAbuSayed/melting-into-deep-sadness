[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the midnight pulling on my soul
Your shadow dancing where the embers glow
I'm melting down into a sweet abyss
Just craving one more taste of your slow kiss

[Verse 1]
The coffee's getting cold on the wooden stand
I'm tracing every line across your hand
The quiet of the room is speaking loud
With just the two of us, we are the crowd

[Pre-Chorus]
There's a fire in the way you look at me
A current underneath a gentle sea
I'm falling deeper every time you sigh
And I don't ever want to say goodbye

[Chorus]
Oh, I am melting into deep sadness when you're gone
But when you're here the night outlasts the dawn
Your velvet touch is all I'm leaning on
My heart is yours to keep, where you belong

[Post-Chorus]

[Verse 2]

A slow intoxication, soft and true

And leave a tender, unforgotten mark

[Bridge]
Oh, I am melting down into your glow

My hands are wrapped around your fragile frame
